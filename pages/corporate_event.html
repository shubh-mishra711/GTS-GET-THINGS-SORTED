We Are...

An experienced corporate events company

Full-service event production, creative and everything in-between—we’ve been doing it all for over 40 years. We serve the greater Atlanta area, southeast region, and cities across the nation. We own our own equipment (which means cost-savings for you) and we have the resources to handle any event you can imagine. Our experience and expertise means you get reliable service every time. And our versatility means you won’t have to work with multiple event planners or service providers. Seriously, we do everything!

A passionate, creative team.

Our Corporate Events Unlimited team members are talented, enthusiastic professionals with an unmatched attention to detail. Each member of our staff will take the time to listen to you, collaborate with you, and answer your questions. Working together, we’ll create an original event, something your guests will remember for years to come.

A partner ready to help you create unique, fun experiences.

Above all, our events are fun. We want to ensure you and your guests have a great time. We spend a lot of time planning and organizing every detail, then executing those details with precision. The result is that our events feel effortless. You and your guests will be able to relax and enjoy themselves at a truly great party.
